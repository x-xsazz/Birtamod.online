1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to birtamod.online ("we," "us," "our," or "Platform"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our multi-vendor marketplace platform.

By using birtamod.online, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Platform.

1.1 About Our Platform

birtamod.online is a multi-vendor marketplace platform operating in Nepal, connecting vendors and buyers across the country. We facilitate transactions between vendors and buyers but do not directly sell products.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Vendor-Specific Information: - Business name and registration details - Tax identification number (PAN) - Bank account information - Government-issued identification documents - Business address and contact information - Business category and description

Buyer Information: - Shipping addresses - Billing addresses - Payment information (processed through third-party payment processors)

Transaction Information: - Purchase history - Order details - Payment amounts - Shipping preferences - Communication with vendors

Communications: - Messages sent through our Platform - Customer support inquiries - Feedback and reviews - Survey responses

User-Generated Content: - Product listings and descriptions - Product images and videos - Reviews and ratings - Comments and forum posts - Profile information

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device and Usage Information: - IP address - Browser type and version - Operating system - Device identifiers - Mobile network information - Pages visited and time spent on pages - Click patterns and navigation paths - Referral URLs - Search queries within the Platform

Location Information: - Approximate location based on IP address - Precise location (if you grant permission) - Shipping and billing addresses

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: - Session cookies - Persistent cookies - Web beacons - Pixel tags - Local storage - Analytics tools

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from: - Social media platforms (if you connect your account) - Payment processors - Shipping and logistics providers - Identity verification services - Marketing partners - Public databases and credit bureaus (for fraud prevention) - Other users (such as when they refer you or leave reviews)

5. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

5.1 Between Vendors and Buyers

When you make a purchase, we share relevant information with the vendor, including: - Your name - Shipping address - Contact information - Order details

Vendors are independent parties and are responsible for their own data practices.

5.2 Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf: - Payment processors (e.g., eSewa, Khalti, banks) - Shipping and logistics companies - Cloud hosting providers - Email service providers - Analytics providers - Customer support tools - Marketing platforms - Security and fraud prevention services - Identity verification services

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

5.3 Advertising Partners

We may share aggregated or de-identified information with advertising partners to: - Display targeted advertisements - Measure ad performance - Conduct market research

We do not sell personally identifiable information to advertisers.

5.4 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified of any such change.

5.5 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or in response to: - Court orders or subpoenas - Law enforcement requests - Government investigations - Legal proceedings - Protection of our rights or property - Emergency situations involving safety

5.6 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you provide explicit consent.

5.7 Public Information

Information you post publicly (such as reviews, comments, or public profile information) may be viewed by anyone.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain your information for as long as necessary to: - Maintain your account - Provide our services - Comply with legal obligations - Resolve disputes - Enforce our agreements

Specific Retention Periods: - Account information: Duration of account plus 3 years after closure - Transaction records: 7 years (for tax and accounting purposes) - Communication records: 3 years - Marketing data: Until you unsubscribe or 2 years of inactivity - Server logs: 90 days

After the retention period, we will delete or anonymize your information unless required by law to retain it longer.
