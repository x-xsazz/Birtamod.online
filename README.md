## What Is This Place

**BIRTAMOD.ONLINE** is Jhapa's local multi-vendor marketplace — where real vendors from Birtamod, Damak, and across the region list products, post services, and connect with buyers who are done waiting for Kathmandu hauls.

No dropshippers. No middlemen taxing your wallet.
Just the actual stuff, from the actual people who sell it, delivered faster than your friend says "aaunu" and never arrives.

Think of it as your local bazaar — but it shows up on your phone at midnight and doesn't judge what you're buying.

**Shop products. Book services. Discover stores.**
All in one place. All local. All yours.
